ABOUT EBC SONSHIP SCHOOL OF THE FIRSTBORN

Sonship School in collaboration with El-Shaddai Bible College is a "school in the church" versus a "school and the church". The concept of the school in the church derives from the thought of certification versus accreditation, where the emphasis is not on scholarship but Sonship and servant-leadership.

  • EBCSS integrates biblical education and service through a worldwide network of extended study programs, making Christian education available whenever and wherever.
  • EBCSS also serves the local church and communities through evangelism, discipleship, counseling and leadership training.

PURPOSE OF SONSHIP SCHOOL

EBCSS (El-Shaddai Bible College and Sonship School) is aimed at individual Christians, ministers of the Gospel, and local assemblies throughout the world. Our focus of Sonship School is certification of able ministers who have been equipped with sound doctrine, and are able to teach others what they have been taught. Everyone is welcome to enroll, but seats are limited.
